About Luna: The Period Tracker

What is Luna?

Luna: The Period Tracker is a comprehensive app designed to help you track and manage your menstrual cycle. It provides useful features and insights to make your period experience more comfortable and empowering.

Key Features

  • Accurate cycle tracking: Log your period start and end dates to track your cycle length and predict future periods.
  • Period reminders: Set customized reminders to help you stay prepared for your upcoming period.
  • Symptom tracking: Record your symptoms, moods, and other relevant information to identify patterns and gain insights into your menstrual health.
  • Health insights: Access educational content and resources related to menstrual health, hygiene, and overall well-being.
  • Analytics and charts: Visualize your cycle data with easy-to-understand charts and analytics to understand your menstrual patterns.
  • Community support: Connect with a community of individuals who share similar experiences and engage in discussions.

Why Choose Luna?

Luna is designed with user convenience and privacy in mind. It offers a user-friendly interface, personalized features, and data protection to ensure a reliable and secure period tracking experience.
