About Luna: The Period Tracker

What is Luna?

Luna: The Period Tracker is a comprehensive app designed to help you track and manage your menstrual cycle. It provides useful features and insights to make your period experience more comfortable and empowering. Log your period start and end dates to track your cycle length and predict future periods.

Why Choose Luna?

  • Luna is your trusted companion on your menstrual journey, providing support and insights every step of the way.
  • With Luna, say goodbye to period surprises and hello to accurate predictions tailored to your unique cycle.
  • Stay informed and empowered with Luna's curated content, offering valuable resources on menstrual health and wellness.
  • With Luna by your side, take control of your menstrual health and embrace your cycle with confidence.
